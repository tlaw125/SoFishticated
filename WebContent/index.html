Hello and welcome to SoFishticated! As you can tell, we cannot get enough of puns! This web app is designed to help new aquarium hobbyists learn the ways of freshwater aquariums and potentially aid experienced ones gain even more knowledge. Fishkeeping is a very fun and relaxing hobby to have and is said to even reduce stress. Coming back home after a long day and watching your fish happily go through the simplicities of life that you help provide it is a very enriching feeling. We sincerely hope this site can help you in any way as you venture on your aquarium journey!
